# Pandas-Challenge

This project includes a detailed analysis of a city's standardized test results & other vital information. 
Observations are noted as comments at the bottom of the notebook.

The breakdown includes:
### District Summary
  * Total Schools
  * Total Students
  * Total Budget
  * Average Math Score
  * Average Reading Score
  * % Passing Math (The percentage of students that passed math.)
  * % Passing Reading (The percentage of students that passed reading.)
  * % Overall Passing (The percentage of students that passed math **and** reading.)

### School Summary
